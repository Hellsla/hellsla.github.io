§ 01 — About

Lauri Hellsten

🇫🇮

I am a STEAM teacher passionate about developing teaching practice. For the past 15 years, hands-on and student-activating pedagogical approaches have been at the heart of my work. As a teacher, I have developed STEAM education, assessment, flipped learning pedagogy, and the meaningful use of ICT in teaching. I have also written and produced a wide range of learning materials for both teachers and students. STEAM outreach and international projects are especially meaningful to me. I believe that together, we are more than the sum of our parts.
