§ 01 — About

Lauri Hellsten

🇫🇮

I am a STEAM teacher at Espoo Yhteislyseo upper secondary school with a strong commitment to developing teaching practice. For the past 15 years, hands-on, student-activating pedagogy has been at the heart of my work.

As a teacher, I have actively developed STEAM education, formative assessment, flipped learning, and the meaningful use of ICT in teaching and learning. I have also written and produced a wide range of learning materials for both teachers and students.

Formative assessment is especially important to me. I aim to support each student within their zone of proximal development by offering feedback, guidance, and appropriately challenging tasks that help them move forward with confidence.

In addition to my teaching role, I serve as the school’s international relations coordinator, working with Erasmus+, Nordplus, and other international projects. STEAM outreach and international collaboration are particularly meaningful to me, as I believe that together we are more than the sum of our parts.

I have also completed the Finnish Educational Administration Qualification, which has strengthened my understanding of school governance, educational legislation, public administration, finance, data protection, and the leadership responsibilities involved in school management.
